About Kertara

Kertara is a collective of individuals united by a shared goal: to build software that prioritizes user privacy and anonymity. We are committed to developing tools that offer robust protection in an increasingly monitored digital world.

Many people assume Kertara is a messaging app or a single product. Actually, Kertara is the name of our community.

We are a group of people from various countries who believe that private conversations on the internet should not be intercepted or sold by third parties. Therefore, we work together to create various privacy protection tools.
