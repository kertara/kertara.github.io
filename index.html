Welcome to Kertara

Kertara is a collective of individuals united by a shared goal: to build software that prioritizes user privacy and anonymity. We are committed to developing tools that offer robust protection in an increasingly monitored digital world.

Recent Announcements

  • May 12, 2026: KeyVault is released for public testing. Please try it and let us know if you find any issues.
  • April 1, 2026: We now have over 500 people helping to write code at Kertara. Thank you!

Why We Exist

Today's internet collects too much of our personal data without permission. We build software that allows you to inspect the code yourself, ensuring your data remains secure. We work transparently and share everything we build for free.
