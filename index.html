Welcome to Kertara

About Kertara

People often ask which app Kertara is, but that’s missing the point. Kertara is the community behind the code. We’re a collective of folks from various backgrounds who share one goal: building software that actually respects your privacy. We don't like how the internet is being monitored, so we’re building tools to fix it. Everything we make is open and built by the community, for the community.

Why We Exist

Modern internet practices shouldn't come at the cost of your privacy. We build fully transparent, open-source software that lets you verify your own security. Our tools are free, auditable, and built for those who refuse to compromise on anonymity.
