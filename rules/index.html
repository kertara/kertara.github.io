Community Rules

To maintain a safe and productive environment, all contributors must adhere to the following rules:

  1. Respect Privacy: Do not attempt to dox or uncover the real identities of anonymous contributors.
  2. No Harassment: We do not tolerate harassment, threats, or discriminatory language in our repositories or communication channels.
  3. Focus on Security: Do not introduce intentional backdoors or obfuscated malware. All code must be clear and auditable.
